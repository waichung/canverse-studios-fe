Introduction
Canverse Studios is for NFT project owners who want to create an integrated social experience with their communities. Most of the social activities surrounding NFT projects are currently online only, and we want to help foster the offline experience through merchandising. With merchandising, projects can also create additional utility around their proprietary tokens. For NFT artists who are adept at their craft but may lack the technical expertise or resources, Canverse Studio helps elevate the social experience for their collectors. Beyond the digital art, artists will be able to reward their collectors with a physical collectible
